# Sentinel

> **The AI Workplace Simulator**

---

## Vision

Sentinel is an AI-powered workplace simulation platform designed to help people prepare for high-pressure professional conversations.

Unlike traditional interview preparation tools that simply ask predefined questions, Sentinel simulates realistic workplace interactions by dynamically adapting its behavior based on the user's responses.

The objective is not to evaluate knowledge alone, but to recreate the emotional pressure, unpredictability, and decision-making found in real interviews and workplace discussions.

---

# Problem

Most interview preparation platforms fail to recreate the psychological aspects of professional conversations.

Existing tools generally:

- Ask static questions
- Provide generic feedback
- Behave politely regardless of response quality
- Never challenge weak or vague answers
- Feel like chatbots instead of interviewers

Real interviews are different.

Interviewers become skeptical.

They interrupt.

They lose interest.

They ask difficult follow-up questions.

They change their tone.

These subtle behavioural shifts are often what determine whether a candidate succeeds or fails.

Sentinel focuses on simulating these interactions.

---

# Solution

Sentinel introduces an adaptive AI interviewer powered by a dynamic behavioural engine.

Rather than following a scripted conversation, the interviewer continuously evaluates user responses and adjusts its personality throughout the interview.

The AI can become:

- Professional
- Supportive
- Skeptical
- Challenging
- Cold
- Encouraging

This creates an interview experience that feels significantly closer to real-world conversations.

---

# Core Concept

Every interview session consists of three phases.

### 1. Preparation

The user configures the interview.

Examples include:

- Role
- Interview Type
- Difficulty
- Interview Style

Future versions will also support resume-aware and job-description-aware interviews.

---

### 2. Simulation

During the interview the AI:

- Asks dynamic questions
- Maintains conversation context
- Generates intelligent follow-up questions
- Adapts behaviour based on answer quality
- Simulates realistic interviewer reactions

The goal is to create immersion rather than simply answering prompts.

---

### 3. Evaluation

After the interview Sentinel generates a detailed feedback report containing:

- STAR framework analysis
- Communication analysis
- Language pattern analysis
- Strengths
- Improvement areas
- Suggested answer rewrites
- Interview summary

The evaluation is intended to provide actionable insights rather than generic scores.

---

# Key Differentiator

The defining feature of Sentinel is its **Adaptive Interview Behaviour Engine**.

Instead of acting as a static chatbot, the interviewer dynamically changes its conversational style based on the quality of the user's responses.

This allows users to experience interviews that feel significantly more realistic and engaging than conventional AI interview platforms.

---

# Target Users

Sentinel is designed for:

- Students
- Fresh Graduates
- Job Seekers
- Professionals preparing for interviews
- Career switchers

Future versions may also support recruiters and internal corporate training.

---

# Goals

The primary goals of Sentinel are:

- Simulate realistic interview experiences
- Improve communication skills
- Encourage structured responses
- Build confidence through deliberate practice
- Deliver meaningful AI-generated feedback

---

# Non-Goals

Sentinel is **not** intended to be:

- A Human Resource Management System (HRMS)
- An Applicant Tracking System (ATS)
- A Resume Builder
- A Generic AI Chatbot
- A Career Portal

Its focus is interview simulation and workplace readiness.
